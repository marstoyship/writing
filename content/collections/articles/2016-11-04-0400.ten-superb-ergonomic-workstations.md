A roundup of workstation setups from across the Lullabot team, framed around the challenge of staying healthy when your job is computer-bound and fully distributed.

An ergonomist observed, the best position is always the next one. From there, nine team members walk through their setups — adjustable-height desks, foam rollers, DIY monitor stands, treadmill desks, and co-working space rotations — alongside a setup from a professional ergonomist for the truly committed. 

The collective message is practical: movement matters more than any single piece of equipment, and the right setup is the one you'll actually change throughout the day.
