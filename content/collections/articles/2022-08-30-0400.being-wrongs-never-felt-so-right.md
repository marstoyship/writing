Clambake, DesignxRI

A short autobiographical talk about a career built on crossed-out plans. 

Starting from art school and a list of things she was definitely going to be — photographer, musician's designer, branding designer, packaging designer, toy designer — Marissa traces the zigzag path that landed her doing UX strategy for state governments serving millions of people. 

The talk is honest about the identity confusion that comes with a hybrid, hard-to-name role ("I'm a, uh... hm..."), and makes the case that every wrong turn was actually load-bearing.
