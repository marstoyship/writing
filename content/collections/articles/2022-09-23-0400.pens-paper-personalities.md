_This group article was originally published on [Lullabot.com](https://www.lullabot.com/resources)._

A roundtable piece collecting the pen, pencil, and notebook preferences of Lullabot team members — with more conviction and specificity than you might expect. The contributions range from fountain pen collections and gel pen sampler experiments to mechanical drafting pencils chosen for lefty smear-resistance. Marissa's section covers her preference for Pentel mechanical pencils, the Leuchtturm1917 dot grid notebook, and the ReMarkable tablet as a distraction-free digital tool. Lightweight in topic, but an honest snapshot of how a distributed team of people who think and communicate visually actually work.
