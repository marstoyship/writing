Live webinar • With Erin Schroeder

Government websites are drowning in PDFs — files that are inaccessible, hard to search, impossible to update, and nearly unusable on mobile. 

We argue that the root cause isn't the PDFs themselves but the processes and habits that keep producing them. 

We cover why PDFs create barriers for users and friction for content teams, how to audit and triage an existing PDF inventory, how to convert PDFs into accessible web content, and how to change internal workflows to stop the cycle from repeating. 

The lessons apply broadly to any large organization publishing high volumes of content.
