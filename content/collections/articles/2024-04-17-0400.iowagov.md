Iowa's 70+ agency websites serve 32 million visitors a year. But behind that traffic was a fragmented ecosystem of siloed governance, inconsistent branding, and content so stale that constituents couldn't find what they needed. My work focused on the human side of a massive platform migration.

> Redundant content cut by 30–90% across agencies. Accessibility scores in the high 90s. Second place, GovTech's Overall State Government Experience award.

I interviewed content authors and public information officers across agencies to surface competing requirements, then prioritized them in collaboration with the state's steering committee. From there I conducted comprehensive content audits and built customized migration plans for every agency, identifying gaps, redundancies, and outdated pages so each launch delivered maximum impact.

The editorial experience was a major focus. I designed the publishing workflows, standardized components, and visual documentation that let agency staff build pages independently without breaking accessibility or design guardrails. Compliance stopped being a separate conversation and became built in.
