30to80 Livestream • Hosted by Bryan Hirsch

A guest appearance on Bryan Hirsch's 30to80 livestream series, covering Lullabot's approach to content audits ahead of large government website redesigns and content migrations. 

The conversation draws on first-hand experience from high-profile state government projects including Mass.gov, Georgia.gov, and Iowa.gov, sharing the process Lullabot uses to assess content at scale — what to look for, which tools actually help, and what the audits reveal that clients rarely anticipate. Hirsch, who worked with Lullabot directly while at Massachusetts Digital Service, brings genuine context to the conversation. 

The result is a practical debrief from someone who has done this work on some of the most complex government sites in the country.
